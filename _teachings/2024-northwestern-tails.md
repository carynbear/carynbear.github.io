I co-designed this course with my advisor, Eleanor O'Rourke, and supported the seminar as a graduate teaching assistant.

The seminar examined the opportunities and risks of modern AI systems for teachers, learners, and learning environments, and asked students to engage directly with AI tools while developing discussion posts, class activities, and a final paper.
